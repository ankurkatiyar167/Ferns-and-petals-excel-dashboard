# Ferns-and-petals-excel-dashboard
This project analyzes sales data from Ferns and Petals (FNP), a company that specializes in delivering gifts for various occasions such as Diwali, Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine's Day, Birthdays, and Anniversaries.

The goal of this project is to analyze customer orders, product performance, and sales trends to generate meaningful insights that can help the business improve its sales strategy and customer satisfaction.

The analysis focuses on identifying patterns in revenue, customer spending behavior, product popularity, and delivery performance.

The main objectives of this analysis are:

- Calculate total revenue generated
- Analyze average order and delivery time
- Study monthly sales trends
- Identify top products by revenue
- Understand customer spending patterns
- Analyze top 5 products performance
- Identify top 10 cities with highest orders
- Study the relationship between order quantity and delivery time
- Compare revenue generated across different occasions
- Identify product popularity based on occasions

Dataset Description
The dataset contains information related to:

* Customer details
* Product information
* Order details
* Delivery dates
* Order quantities
* Sales revenue
* Occasions and festivals

These variables help analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.

Tools and Technologies Used
Excel  – Data visualization and dashboard creation along with pivot table and power query.
Key Insights

Some important insights derived from the analysis include:

- Identification of high revenue generating products
- Understanding seasonal sales trends
- Recognizing cities with highest order volumes
- Observing customer spending behavior
- Understanding which products perform better during specific occasions

Dashboard Features
The dashboard includes the following visualizations:

 KPI
- Total Revenue
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Top 5 Products by Revenue
- Top 10 Cities by Orders
- Occasion-wise Revenue Comparison
- Customer Spending Analysis
- Order Quantity vs Delivery Time Analysis
  


This analysis helps the business:

- Optimize product strategy
- Improve delivery efficiency
- Identify high performing cities
- Plan marketing campaigns around occasions
- Improve customer experience

This project demonstrates how data analysis and visualization can help businesses gain insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. By leveraging data-driven insights, companies can make better strategic decisions and improve overall performance.
